About

We Love hosting guests in our luxury B&B Accommodation

Mabel Estate self-catered accommodation makes it easy to soak in all the sights, flavours and colour of the Beach & Hills.

Offering bed & breakfast accommodation in the beautiful Henley Beach.

At Mabel Estate, we've blended the calming coastal vibes with the inviting comfort of a self-contained villa. Mabel Estate was once an old fisherman's haven, reborn in 2015 into a cozy retreat that whispers the serenity of the nearby waves.

Mabel Estate encapsulates the best of hills & beach, we invite you to savor the allure of the coast and hills. Experience the captivating seaside tranquility while being a heartbeat away from the charming hillside landscape, creating unforgettable memories at our beachside sanctuary.

We eagerly await the opportunity to host you at Mabel Estate, where the beach and hills embrace, providing you with a personalised retreat at the edge of both worlds.
